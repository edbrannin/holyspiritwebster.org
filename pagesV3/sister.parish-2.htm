Sister Parish News

 

October/05/2008
Beginning with this month of October, the Holy Hour on the second Friday of each month will be dedicated to our sister parish in the Ivory Coast. I would like to invite as many parishioners who can do so, to join the group of faithful people who spend an hour before the Blessed Sacrament and offer their prayer for the Christian Community of Our Lady of Africa in Abidjan, Ivory Coast.
I take this opportunity to remind anyone who wishes to offer a story, a good wish, a prayer, or a photo for the Parish Scrapbook being prepared by Carolyn King as a gift to send to our sister parish. There is a box in the church foyer where any item listed above can be deposited for the scrapbook. It would be nice if many parishioners would participate in this project. Thank you.
Fr. Michael Costanzo

November/02/2008
PROJECT No. 1 came to its full completion when the Holy Spirit BANNER was placed in the Church of Our Lady of Africa on Sunday October 19, 2008. Hopefully we will receive some photos in the near future. ACTIVITY No. 1 started on the second Friday of this month of October, when those present at the regularly scheduled Holy Hour prayed for the parishioners of Our Lady of Africa. PROJECT No.2 - the making of a Holy Spirit Church SCRAPBOOK to send to our sister parish - is underway. You can continue to contribute photos old and new, brief saying, or other memorabilia. Drop them in the box set for this project in our church foyer. PROJECT No.3 begins this Sunday. Barb Trottier (872-5282) will place a box in the church foyer to collect Christmas ornaments to be sent to our sister parish. The package will be shipped at the beginning of December. Please drop off what you wish to donate ASAP. Many Thanks to everyone.
Fr. Michael Costanzo

November/09/2008
To join Project #3, use your own talents by making a non-breakable Christmas ornament for Our Lady of Africa’s parish tree or purchase one from our crafty crew to fill the box in our church gathering area. Please call Marianne Schonning at 787-0281 for more details.

December/07/2008
We have now completed our Christmas Decorations Project. The collected items will be shipped to our sister parish during this coming week. This month we begin our second spiritual activity: PRAYING ONE HOUR BEFORE THE BLESSED SACRAMENT for members of Our Lady of Africa Parish Community. You will find special cards on the table at the entrance of the church, which you can take a few moments to fill after you have spent an hour before the Blessed Sacrament at your convenience. You will also find the names of members of Our Lady of Africa to select before you begin the adoration. These cards will then be collected at the end of each month and sent to the pastor of Our Lady of Africa who will distribute them to the individuals for whom you prayed. Thank you for participating in this beautiful activity.

January/04/2009
This month of January we begin our 4th Sister Parish Project: It's PEN PAL time. If you are interested, please contact me at 872-5039 or Vinnie Amuso. Also, we begin this month something special for our own parish that will ultimately benefit not only ourselves but our country as well (and later also our sister parish).

We will hold on the Wednesday after the first Sunday of each month a special holy hour before our Lord in the church from 7-8 PM based on the prayerful reading of a brief selection from the Holy Scriptures followed by meditation. It's a very old Christian practice called Lectio Divina. Flyers are available at the church entrance for anyone who wishes to know more. We invite you to join us and enjoy God's presence.

January/11/2009
Our 2 SPIRITUAL ACTIVITIES during this year are:
1. Join other members of our parish in prayer during the Holy Hour (8-9PM) on the second Friday of each month.
2. Offer a Holy Hour at your own time for a person in need either from our parish or from Our Lady of Africa, and fill out the prayer card provided. The first group of prayer cards have already been sent to our Sister Parish.
Fr. Michael Costanzo

January/25/2009
Today (Sunday, Jan. 25th) both Holy Spirit Church and Our Lady of Africa Church representatives are signing during Mass, an official certificate recognizing their mutual status of Sister Parishes. We invite all parishioners to keep both parishes in their prayers and to become involved, according to each individual's choice, in some of the projects and activities that take place during the year. Many blessings are showered on all members of the two churches through a strengthened commitment of each individual believer to the Lord Jesus and his universal Church. Shalom and Joy.

February/1/2009
We are ready to begin a PEN PAL correspondence program with members of our Sister Parish Our Lady of Africa. We the parishioners of Holy Spirit will initiate the program by sending letters to students at College Notre Dame D' Afrique de Bietry, which is the High School affiliated with our Sister Parish. We are asking Holy Spirit Parishioners who wish to participate to write a letter to a student at this High School. Please drop the letters off at the sacristy or send them to me, Vinny Amuso, at 83 Angels Path, Webster, NY 14580 and I will make sure they are sent off to the Ivory Coast. If you have any questions please feel free to call me at 787-1666 or email me at vjaeee@rit.edu
Vinny Amuso
