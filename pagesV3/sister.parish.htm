Sister Parish News

September/07/2008
Our first Parish Project, linking our parish family to the Christian Community of Our Lady of Africa in Abidjan, Ivory Coast, was the making of a Holy Spirit Church. banner which is on its way to our sister Parish in Africa.
I would like to thank Marianne Schonning for her full commitment of time and resources to this project, and, with her, Kayla and Stephanie, who worked with her: my heartfelt gratitude.
I hope you saw the beautiful banner placed by the organ during last weekend masses.
Our second Parish Project will begin now and continue for several weeks. Carolyn King has offered to coordinate our efforts to make a PARISH LIFE SCRAPBOOK that will eventually be sent to our sister parish. You may call her (671-2127) if you wish to help in any way.
Items needed vary from First Communion pictures to wedding photos, from parish grounds photos to parish community news, personal wishes, brief family narratives, etc.
I invite all of you to participate according to your preference. Thank you.
Fr. Michael Costanzo
