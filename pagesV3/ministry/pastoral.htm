PASTORAL AND SACRAMENTAL

Sacramental Preparation

Marriage - Notify the Rectory at least nine months prior to the wedding date. Contact: Rectory Shari Magagnoli 671-5520

Baptism - offers instruction to those who wish to have infants baptized in our parish. This training is provided on an individual basis and at a mutually agreeable time. Call at least one month before required date. Contact: Pastoral Associate Deacon Brian Mahoney 671-8317

Children's Faith Formation - Preparing children for First Communion, Penance and Confirmation is part of our Faith Formation Program. Contact: Faith Formation Coordinator Tony Ferrera 671-5680

Rite of Christian Initiation for Adults (RCIA) - offers formation and instruction for possible reception into the Catholic Church. Meetings begin in the fall and continue until the Easter Vigil on Holy Saturday evening. Contact: Pastoral Associate Deacon Brian Mahoney 671-8317

Pastoral Care

Sacrament of the Sick - Contact: Rectory

Communion To The Sick and Homebound - ministers make regular visits to Hill Haven Nursing Home to take Holy Communion to residents who request it. These visits are made on a weekly (or bi-weekly) basis at a mutually agreeable time. Ministers are trained both by the parish and Hill Haven. Contact: Pastoral Associate Deacon Brian Mahoney 671-8317

Funeral - Contact: Rectory. Families may gather in the parish hall after a funeral for coffee and/or lunch - under the direction of Bereavement/Funeral Ministry. Shari Magagnoli 671-5520
