SOCIAL MINISTRY

Social Ministry Committee - Our purpose is to fulfill the request of Jesus - 'What you do for the least of my brothers you do for me.' Parishioner donations presently being coordinated include: 'Have a Heart Weekend' and 'Bag-a-Meal' - for distribution of personal items and food by Hope House, Easter Baskets, Baby Shower - for distribution of infant items by Birthright, 'Angel Tree' - Christmas requests from children 1-10 years of age. Monthly funding is provided to various charitable organizations within our community including: Daystar, Problem Pregnancy Help Center, and St. Martin's Place. We pick up and deliver surplus food to distribution centers, i.e. House of Mercy and St. Peter and Paul Church on a regular basis and oversee a special fund for unexpected emergencies. Sympathy, get well and thinking-of-you cards are mailed as needed. Social Ministry includes Bereavement/Funeral ministry.

Bereavement/Funeral Ministry - This offers an opportunity for families to gather in the parish hall after a funeral for coffee and/or lunch. We provide coffee and desserts on request, or set up food and assist with funeral receptions. Shari Magagnoli 671-5520

St. Clement of Rome Committee - This newly formed Committee addresses how our own parish can play a role in the recovery efforts for St. Clement of Rome Parish in Louisiana. The Committee acts in conjunction with the three other Webster parishes to foster practical support that includes financial assistance (fundraisers), spiritual support (prayers), renovation help (volunteer work weeks at the parish) and emotional support (letters, banners, and ministry pairing).

Social Ministry Assistance Groups

Health Ministry - serves in the area of personal wellness. It is a resource for health information, offering a blood pressure clinic, health education programs and library. Sites are rotated among the 4 parishes of the WPCC. Carol Rutigliano.
