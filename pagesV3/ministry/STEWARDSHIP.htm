STEWARDSHIP

Stewardship Committee - This new committee will function in direct communication with the Pastor and Pastoral Council to plan, coordinate, and implement an ongoing stewardship process and resource development plan for the parish. Overall objectives include (1) supporting parish's daily and priority needs; (2) educating and facilitating efforts to re-invigorate parishioners on collaborative stewardship, to be a good steward of the gifts we are given, calling forth many talents and gifts from our parish community; (3) ensuring lasting and effective spiritual/physical/financial growth of the parish.
