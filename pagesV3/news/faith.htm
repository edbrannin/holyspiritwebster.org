Rite of Christian Initiation of Adults
RCIA is the process through which non-Catholics and Catholics who are not fully initiated into the Catholic faith become full members of the Church.
If you or someone you know, would like to know more about the Catholic Faith, please call Deacon Brian at 671-8317.

Lectors NEEDED!
Please consider becoming a lector. We find ourselves in need of men and women to proclaim the Word as regularly scheduled lectors and lectors that can sub on occasion. If you are interested in volunteering or learning more about this ministry, please contact Deacon Brian at 671-8317 at your earliest convenience.

Children’s Holy Half Hour Saturdays 3:00-3:30

Diocesan Prayer to the Holy Spirit
Holy Spirit, Loving Spirit of God, Spirit of Christ,
Touch our Hearts. Lead us, Guide us, Refresh us, heal us, and forgive us. Give us new life. We make this prayer for ourselves and all the people of our Diocese. Be with us in these months with your life, your love and your encouragement Holy Spirit Touch us, Be with us always
We pray through Christ our Lord. Amen.

Vocation Prayer
Lord, let me know clearly the work,which you are calling me to do in life. Grant me the grace and generosity I need to answer your call with courage and love, for to do your will is my joy and my peace.
Amen

Stewardship Prayer
My church is composed of people like me. I help make it what it is. It will be friendly, if I am. Its pews will be filled, if I help fill them. It will do great work, if I work. It will make generous gifts to many causes, if I am a generous giver. It will bring other people into its worship and fellowship, if I invite and bring them. It will be a church of loyalty and love, of fearlessness and faith, and a church with a noble spirit, if I, who make it what it is, am filled with these same things. Therefore, with the help of God, I shall dedicate myself to the task of being all the things that I want my church to be. Amen.
