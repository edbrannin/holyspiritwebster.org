Helping Hope House this Easter
Easter will be here soon and again we will be distributing baskets of food to the food cupboard at Holy Trinity Church (HOPE House). Baskets are available for you in the gathering area of the church. We encourage you to take a basket, place it in your kitchen and fill it with food. The basket in the kitchen is intended to bring to mind the acts of sacrifice and sharing.

Please bring the baskets filled with food and paper products to the church on or before the weekend of March 29.

Why do people join any group or organization? Camaraderie? Financial benefits? Activities? Causes? Assistance? Religion? The inability to say \no!.?
Because people are different, it can be difficult to pinpoint way a person is drawn to a particular group, but something always motivates a person to join.
Whatever the motivations the Knights of Columbus offers a man the opportunity to play a significant role in building a better world. Membership in the order provides a vehicle to do something positive locally and to have an impact internationally. K of C membership gives a person the chance to champion causes in his parish and community, and to have the resources available to be involved throughout his state and the world.
The most common reason why men have not joined the K of C is that they have never been asked!
