Homebound ?
If you have a family member or know of someone in our parish who is homebound and would like to receive Holy Communion, please call the parish office at (671-5520).

Hospitalized ?
Because of patient privacy rules, hospitals are not able to give out names of patients over the phone, unless you have given them permission. Therefore, if your or a loved one is hospitalized and would like a priest to visit, please call the parish office (671-5520).

Additional Information

Planning Events and Activities ?
Everyone who is planning an event, it is imperative that you check the availability of the parish center and gathering area before planning /scheduling an event. This avoids conflicts with other planned activities for available space; both in the Parish center and Church gathering area. Contact: Shari Magagnoli 671-5520 to reserve space for your activity.

Mass Card Intentions
If you receive a Holy Spirit Mass card with no scheduled date noted on the card for the Mass intention, you need to call the parish office as soon as possible if you wish to schedule a specific date. Otherwise, we will schedule the intention where an opening occurs. Mass intentions are listed in our bulletin every week for the upcoming week and can serve as a reminder if you are planning to attend the Mass.
Our Mass book is now open, and requests will be accepted during office hours M-F 8:00-4:00. Stipends are requested at the time of scheduling. We ask that you please limit your requests for weekend Masses to no more than 4 intentions, so that we may be able to fulfill all requests. Before you arrive, it would helpful if you would have your requests written out, including:

  1. Dates and times requested
  2. Name of deceased
  3. Name of requester.

Prayer Requests Reminder
If you ask one of our priests or staff to add someone to the prayer list, please leave your name also. We will then be able to follow up with you regarding the progress of those who are ill and when they may be removed from the prayer list. Please call the office at 671-5520 if you (or your loved one’s name) would like to remain on the list.

Parish Council Mailbox
If you have questions, suggestions or comments, please feel free to use the Parish Council comment box on the parish table in the gathering area. There is also an on-line form available here. Comments are reviewed at the Parish Council monthly meeting.
